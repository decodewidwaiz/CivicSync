# CivicSync – Smart Local Issue Reporting Platform

## Background

**CivicSync** is a centralized web and mobile-based platform that enables citizens to report local civic issues such as potholes, garbage overflow, streetlight failures, drain blockages, and water leakages.

The platform promotes transparency, faster issue resolution, and efficient municipal management through real-time tracking and role-based dashboards.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

In many cities, citizens face challenges when reporting local infrastructure problems due to:

* Lack of a centralized digital platform
* Manual complaint submission processes
* Absence of real-time complaint tracking
* Delayed issue resolution
* Limited transparency and accountability

As a result, civic issues often remain unresolved for long periods, affecting the quality of life of residents.

---

## 🎯 Solution Overview

**CivicSync** provides a one-stop digital solution that empowers citizens and local authorities through:

### 📸 Photo-Based Complaint Submission

Users can upload images of civic issues for accurate reporting and verification.

### 📍 Automatic GPS Location Detection

The platform automatically captures the issue location, reducing manual effort and improving accuracy.

### 🗂 Categorized Issue Reporting

Citizens can report issues under predefined categories such as:

* Potholes
* Garbage Overflow
* Streetlight Failures
* Water Leakages
* Drain Blockages
* Road Damage
* Public Safety Concerns

### 🔄 Real-Time Status Tracking

Users can monitor complaint progress through different stages:

* Submitted
* Assigned
* In Progress
* Resolved

### 📊 Admin Dashboard

Municipal administrators can:

* View all complaints
* Assign tasks to workers
* Monitor resolution timelines
* Analyze issue trends and performance metrics

### 👷 Worker Panel

Field workers receive assigned tasks and can:

* Update work status
* Upload resolution evidence
* Mark issues as completed

**CivicSync** aims to bridge the gap between citizens and municipal authorities by creating a transparent, efficient, and technology-driven civic issue management system that improves urban living standards and strengthens public trust.
